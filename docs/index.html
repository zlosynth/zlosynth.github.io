Hailing from Brno, Czechia, Zlosynth Instruments is a project which aims to create eurorack modules that allow access to musical harmony with a healthy dose of noisy insanity.

About

My name is Petr. I played flute and trumpet as a kid, drums as an adolescent and guitar as an adult. I have been a computer nerd ever since the flute times. And I have always enjoyed creating stuff. During the pandemic in 2020, despite not knowing much about embedded systems, being mediocre at music theory and subpar at maths, I have decided to have some good time designing and assembling my own eurorack module.
