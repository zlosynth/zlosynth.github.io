Hailing from Brno, Czechia, Zlosynth Instruments is a project which aims to provide intuitive and immersive eurorack modules that allow access to both musical harmony and insanity.

Achordion

Chord-crafting quantizing wavetable oscillator.

About

My name is Petr. I played flute and trumpet as a kid, drums as an adolescent and guitar as an adult. I have been a computer nerd ever since the flute times. And I have always enjoyed building stuff. During the pandemic in 2020, despite not knowing anything about embedded systems, being mediocre at music theory and subpar at maths, I have decided to have some good time designing and assembling my own eurorack module.
